About Us

Aždaja doo is a company from Serbia founded in 2008 that specializes in production, import, and wholesale of aquarium fish.
We import a wide range of aquarium fish from Singapore, Thailand, Israel, and Indonesia. Our collaboration with reliable suppliers allows us to offer a diverse selection of exotic fish species that may not be readily available in the local environment. We carefully select the fish for import to ensure that they are healthy, well-adapted, and meet our established standards.
In addition to imported fish, Aždaja doo provides customers with a wide range of fish from our own breeding facilities.
Through our expertise and extensive experience, we ensure that our fish are healthy, vibrant, and ready for their new homes in our customers' aquariums.
